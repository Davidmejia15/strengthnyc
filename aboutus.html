About Us:

An Organization that spreads Love and Powerful messages through clothing.

StrengthNYC was established in April 24 of 2016 in the Bronx, NY by founder David Mejia.

David was inspired by God to create this clothing line to spread the word of God and empower his people.

God gave David a vision to seek an organization to partner with and donate a portion of his profits to the Homeless.
